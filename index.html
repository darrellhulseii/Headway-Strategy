Why Nextdoor First?

Nextdoor is a trust-based platform where neighbors recommend local services to each other. A therapist ad on Nextdoor reads as a community resource — not an intrusion. CPL of $12–18 is 30–40% lower than any other paid channel for local therapists. Start here.

Expected Results at $75/mo
Profile visits: 4–7 / month
Booking rate: 18–22%
New clients: 1–2 / month
CPL: ~$15
Client LTV: $2,400

Setup: Neighborhood Sponsorship

In Nextdoor Ads Manager, select "Neighborhood Sponsorship." Choose 2–3 neighborhoods within 5 miles of your practice. $75 covers 1–2 neighborhoods per month. This places your business in the right-column sidebar of the main neighborhood feed — prime visibility during the 6–9pm window when Nextdoor usage peaks.

Targeting Settings
Ad type: Neighborhood Sponsorship
Neighborhoods: Your 2–3 closest
Radius: 5 miles from your office
Schedule: Always on (no time restriction)
Peak hours: 6pm–9pm weekdays auto-boost
Destination: Your Headway UTM link

Ad Copy — Community Framing (Not Clinical)

Write this as a neighbor talking to neighbors. No clinical language. No urgency tactics. Lead with accessibility — the fact that most insurance is accepted through Headway removes the biggest objection (cost) before they've even clicked. This is the single most effective angle for Nextdoor MFT ads.

✦ Nextdoor Ad Copy — Use This Exactly
Headline: Licensed Marriage & Family Therapist in [Your Neighborhood]

Body: I'm [First Name], an MFT a few miles away in [City]. I see individuals, couples, and families — and I accept most major insurance through Headway, which means you pay only your copay.

I have openings this month. See my full profile and availability at the link below.

CTA: View Profile → [Headway UTM link]

Organic Posts — Monthly (Free)

Pair the paid ad with one organic business post per month. This doesn't cost extra and compounds reach. Keep the organic post conversational — think of it as a friendly neighborhood update, not marketing. Organic Nextdoor posts from local therapists average 3–6 genuine DM inquiries per post.

✦ Monthly Organic Post — Copy This

Why Google Search?

Google Search captures people actively typing "therapist near me" or "couples counseling in [your city]." These are your hottest prospects — they've already decided they want therapy, they're just choosing who. CPL is higher ($38–50) but booking rates are the highest of any channel (22–30%). Even $75/mo generates meaningful results.

Expected Results at $75/mo
Clicks to profile: 18–30 / month
Booking rate: 22–28%
New clients: 1–2 / month
Avg CPL: $42
CPC target: $2.50–4.00

Campaign Setup — Exact Match Only

Create one campaign: "Local MFT Search." One ad group with 5–8 exact match keywords. Do NOT use broad match — it will waste your budget on searches like "free online therapy app." Exact match keeps every dollar on genuinely interested local searchers.

Keywords — Copy These Exactly
[marriage counselor {your city}]
[couples therapist near me]
[marriage and family therapist {city}]
[family therapist {city}]
[couples counseling {city}]
[MFT {city}]
[therapist accepting {insurance name}]
Negative keywords — add these: "free" · "online app" · "BetterHelp" · "Talkspace" · "crisis" · "hotline" · "emergency" · "jobs" · "degree" · "coursework"

Call Extension — Don't Skip This

Many people searching for a therapist prefer to call before booking online. A phone call extension adds your number directly to the ad. Callers convert to booked clients at 2–3× the rate of clicks. Even a few calls per month from this $75 budget can double your new client intake.

Call Extension Setup
Go to: Google Ads → Assets → Call Asset
Phone number: Your direct line
Schedule: Mon–Sat 9am–8pm only
Call reporting: ON (track call length)
Calls > 60 seconds = warm lead

Note: Add sitelink asset too — link directly to your Headway profile and label it "See My Availability."

What Meta Does That Others Can't

Google catches people who are actively searching. Meta reaches people who aren't searching yet — but who are exactly the right demographic. It builds your pipeline of future clients and drives awareness that makes your Google ads convert better when they eventually search. Add this in month 2 or 3 once your Google campaign is profitable.

Expected Results at $150/mo
Profile visits: 150–280 / month
Booking rate: 10–16%
New clients: 2–4 / month
Avg CPV: $0.55–1.10
Blended CPL: ~$24

Campaign Setup — Traffic Objective

Use "Traffic" objective — not Leads. You want people going TO your Headway profile to book there, not filling out a Meta form. Set destination URL to your Headway UTM link. One campaign, one ad set, 2–3 creative variations to test. Keep it simple.

Campaign Structure
Objective: Traffic (Link Clicks)
Destination: Headway UTM link
Budget type: Daily ($5/day)
Placements: Automatic (Meta recommends)
Ad set: 1 (start simple)
Creatives: 2–3 to A/B test
Bidding: Cost per click (CPC)

Compliant Targeting — No Health Conditions

Meta prohibits targeting based on mental health conditions. You cannot target "depression" or "anxiety." What you CAN do is target life events that correlate with therapy-seeking — recently married, recently moved, newly engaged — plus broad demographic + interest layers. This is still highly effective and fully compliant.

Targeting Setup
Location: 15-mile radius, your city
Age: 26–52
Life Events: Recently Married, Newly Engaged, Recently Moved
Interests: Self-improvement, Relationships, Parenting, Mindfulness, Personal development
Exclude: Your existing clients (upload email list)
Do NOT use: Any mental health-related interest categories

Creative — Your Face Converts 2–3× Better

A warm, real photo of you consistently outperforms stock therapy imagery (hands, couch, abstract art). The ad is essentially a first impression of what it's like to work with you. Before anyone reads a word, your photo communicates approachability, warmth, and credibility. Use a natural-light photo with a genuine expression.

Creative Specs
Format: 1×1 square image (1080×1080px)
Subject: YOU — warm, approachable headshot
OR: Your office — natural light, inviting
NOT: Stock photos of strangers or abstract art
Text overlay (optional): "Now accepting new clients"
Placement: Facebook Feed + Instagram Feed
Video option: 15-sec authentic intro video

Test these three copy angles. Run each for 2 weeks with equal budget. The winner gets the rest of your monthly budget. The "insurance accessibility" angle consistently wins for Headway therapists because it removes cost anxiety — the #1 reason people don't start therapy.

✦ Meta Ad Copy — 3 Angles (Test These)

Most people don't realize their insurance probably covers therapy. I'm a licensed MFT in [City] and I use Headway for billing — so you pay your copay, not out-of-pocket rates. I have openings this month. [Link]

$0 EXTRA COST 2–3× MORE BOOKINGS Ongoing

The 5-Minute Response Rule

Respond to every inquiry — phone, email, or Headway message — within 5 minutes during business hours. Response rate drops from 40% to 7% after just 2 hours. Set up a Headway notification on your phone and treat the first response as your most important task of the day.

✦ First Response Script — Use This
Hi [Name] — thank you for reaching out. I'm [First Name], and I'd love to connect.

I have availability [this week / next week] — you can see my open times and book directly at [your Headway link].

If you have any questions before scheduling, feel free to reply here and I'll get back to you quickly.

Looking forward to hearing from you,
[Your Name], LMFT

If They Don't Book — 3-Day Follow-Up

Many people who inquire genuinely intend to book but get busy or hesitant. A single follow-up message sent 3 days later recovers 20–25% of non-bookers. Keep it warm and low-pressure — one sentence removing the main barrier (usually cost or scheduling) plus a direct link.

✦ 3-Day Follow-Up Script — Use This
Hi [Name] — just wanted to follow up in case my earlier note got buried.

I still have availability [this week / next week]. If cost was a concern, my Headway profile lets you check your exact insurance copay before you commit to anything — usually $20–50/session depending on your plan.

No pressure at all — just wanted to make sure you had the info. [Headway link]

Warmly,
[Your Name]

After the First Session — Referral Ask

After a client's 3rd session, they're the most likely to refer someone they know. A simple, genuine question after session generates 20–30% of new clients for established therapists — at zero ad cost. Build this into your standard session wrap-up.

✦ Referral Script — End of Session 3
"I'm really glad this has been helpful. I do have a few openings for new clients — if you ever have a friend or family member who's been thinking about therapy and might be a good fit for my approach, feel free to share my Headway profile with them.

It's [your Headway link] — they can check my availability and their insurance coverage right there."
